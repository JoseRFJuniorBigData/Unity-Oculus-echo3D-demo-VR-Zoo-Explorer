Welcome to our VR Zoo! Head on in and enjoy the attraction. 
You'll be able to play 5 minigames in the zoo by talking to tour guides standing in front of specific animal exhibits. 
You can play the minigame by approaching them and they'll start talking to you. 
If you continue talking with the tour guide, you will eventually start the minigame.
If you wish to not play the minigame, simply walk away from the tour guide. 
Enjoy!
